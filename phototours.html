Photo Tours
No fixed route.

Every photo tour I run is fully customised and private. I build each experience around your interests, your skill level, and the kind of images you want to make — not a fixed itinerary that I take every client through.

I plan each day with flexibility, responding to light, unfolding situations, and real opportunities as they happen. If the morning calls for staying longer in a particular lane, we stay. If the afternoon opens something unexpected, we follow it.

After more than a decade working with international photographers across India, I bring both deep local knowledge and the practical fluency of knowing how to photograph here — navigating busy streets, engaging with people respectfully, and guiding you throughout without interrupting the experience.

Private 1-on-1 Workshops · Kolkata
The city is your
classroom.

Kolkata is one of the most rewarding cities in the world for street photography. It is layered, unpredictable, and constantly alive — and working within it is itself the education.

In my workshops, you don't just photograph the city — you experience it from within. From early mornings in the flower markets to quieter, intimate moments unfolding in narrow lanes, I'll show you how to work inside these environments, not simply observe them from a distance.

I never focus on what to shoot. My focus is on helping you learn how to see — how to anticipate moments, build compositions, and respond to light and movement in real time, while staying true to your own way of working.

Over the past 10+ years, I've worked with more than 500 photographers through workshops and photo tours — many of them travelling to India for the first time. That experience means I can guide you through both the photographic and cultural dimensions of working here, helping you adapt, connect, and photograph with ease.
