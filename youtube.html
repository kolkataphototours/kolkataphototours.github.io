My YouTube channel is dedicated to street photography in Kolkata and is an extension of the street photography tours and photography workshops I personally lead in the city.

Photographing Kolkata with Shivam is a focused series that explores the exact locations where I host my street photography workshops, from bustling markets and historic neighbourhoods to quiet lanes filled with everyday life.

In each episode, I share practical street photography techniques, real-time shooting decisions, and insights into approaching strangers, working with natural light, and finding strong compositions in unpredictable environments.
