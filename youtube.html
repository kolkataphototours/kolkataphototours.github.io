Photographing Kolkata with Shivam

My YouTube channel is dedicated to street photography in Kolkata and is an extension of the tours and workshops I personally lead in the city.Photographing Kolkata with Shivam explores the exact locations where I host workshops — from bustling markets to quiet lanes filled with everyday life.In each episode, I share practical street photography techniques, real-time shooting decisions, and insights into approaching strangers, working with natural light, and finding strong compositions in unpredictable environments. If you’re interested in learning street photography in Kolkata, this series offers both inspiration and hands-on guidance from the field.
